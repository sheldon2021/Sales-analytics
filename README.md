# 📊 Sales Analytics – Customer Segmentation & Revenue Analysis

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce transaction data to evaluate business performance and customer behavior using:

- Revenue & KPI Analysis
- RFM Customer Segmentation
- Cohort Retention Analysis
- Power BI Dashboard Visualization

---

## 🧠 Business Objectives

- Identify high-value customers
- Understand revenue concentration
- Measure retention trends
- Detect churn-risk segments
- Analyze monthly revenue growth

---

## 📊 Key Metrics Calculated

- Total Revenue  
- Total Orders  
- Average Order Value (AOV)  
- Average Delivery Time  
- RFM Scores (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)

---

## 🏆 Customer Segments Identified

- Champions  
- Loyal Customers  
- At Risk Customers  
- Others  

---

## 🔁 Cohort Retention Insights

- Significant drop-off after first purchase month
- Loyal and Champion segments drive majority revenue
- Revenue concentration follows Pareto distribution
